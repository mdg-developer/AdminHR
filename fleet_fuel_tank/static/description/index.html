How to Create Fuel Tank

You can create Fuel Tank Go To Fleet > Configuration > Fuel Tank.


Add Fuel Wizard

When you click on Add Fuel button then new wizard will be open, In that wizard you have to enter fuel volume and fuel price as your requirement.


Fuel Tank View

After entering fuel details you can see below fields filled with value.

Average price and Total Filling Fuel will be calculate based on fuel volume and price.

In Fuel Filling History, Created fuel history will be display here.


Create Vehicle Fuel Logs

You can create vehicle fuel logs.Select Fuel Tank which is created from Fuel Tank also enter fuel volume, price and set odometer details.


Fuel Consumption Details

After creating vehicle fuel logs, Last Odometer, Consumption Average and Consumption History will be recorded.


Fuel Tank View

After managing vehicle fuel logs, Selected fuel volume will be deducted from used fuel volume.
